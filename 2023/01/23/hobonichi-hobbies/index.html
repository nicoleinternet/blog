how I ended up loving my diary!!

dog

I was never really a person that liked using my diary. But here is a really sweet memory that reminds me of why I use it!

Heinz is my dog, a (currently) 1 year and 4 month old Keeshond. He’s super fluffy, and his breed is originally from the Netherlands. I honestly wouldn’t have been able to keep all the information and obsess over his every details, such as his specific breed markings and coat needs, if it wasn’t for my breeder in Queensland who made me write everything down.

When I started journalling again, I made sure to track this all down in it with a journal I liked!

diary recc

I got heavily into the hobonichi series purely because it was massively cute. I still have the same Doraemon cover I’ve had from two years ago and strive to enter into it every day. If you haven’t seen them, you should look at hobonichi at their website. They also make one of my favourite apps to track my animals at, どこのこ (dokonoko, where are you in Japanese). They have a lot of cute one-shots here about pets as well, from excerpts of pet owners diaries.

Diaries can sometimes feel genuinely draining to do at the end of the day or at the start of the morning when things feel foggy, but they are so rewarding to reminisce on. They say a picture writes a thousand words, but I also reckon that a diary entry is very specific on how you felt in that time as your memory rewrites itself over and over. SO, I really reccomend doing it! This is aaalso a test of Hexo for me!
